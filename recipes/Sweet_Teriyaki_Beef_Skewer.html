Sweet Teriyaki Beef Skewers

Description

A grilled and easy way to prepare a delightful beef appetizer meat dish! Kids live the tenderness and swetness,as do adults

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups light brown sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup pineaplle juice (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large garlic cloves, chopped
  • 4 pounds boneless round steak, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • bamboo skewers soaked in water

Steps

  1. Whisk brown sugar, soy sauce, pineaplle juice, water, vegetabl oil, and garlic together in a large bowl; drop beef slices into mixture and stir to coat. Cover bowl with plastic wrap
  2. Marinate beed in refridgerator for 24 hours
  3. Remove the beef from the marinade, shaking to remove excess lquid. Discard marinade
  4. Thread beef slices in a zig-zag onto the skewers
  5. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate
  6. Cook skewers on preheated grill until the beef is cooked through, about 3 miuntes per side
