Air Fried Chicken

Description

A quick delicious dish of crispy chciken that doesn't need oil

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sweet parpika
  • 1/2 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 8 chicken wings, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F ( 175 degrees C)
  2. Whisk together brown sugar, paprika, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, poultry seasoning, mustard powder, and pepper in a large bowl
  3. Toss in chicken wings and rub seasonings into chicen with your hands until fully coated
  4. Arrange wings in the air fryer basket, standing them up on their ends and leaning against each other and the wall of the basket
  5. Cook in the preheated air fryer until wings are tender inside and golden brown and crisp on the outside, about 35 minutes. Transfer wings to a plate and serve hot
